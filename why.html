? Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of content can I submit?

We accept photos and videos from your Iceland travels — landscapes, attractions, activities, accommodations, restaurants, and anything that captures your experience. The higher the quality, the better!

Do I lose ownership of my content?

No! You retain full ownership of your photos and videos. By submitting, you grant Airmango a non-exclusive license to use your content for our platform, marketing, and travel guides.

How do I receive my Bubble Hotel gift certificate?

Once your content is reviewed and approved, we'll send your Bubble Hotel gift certificate (worth €300) to the email address you provided when uploading. This typically takes 5-7 business days after approval.

What are the content requirements?

Photos should be at least 200KB in size. Videos can be up to 1GB. All content must be your original work, and you must own the rights to share it.

Can I submit content from multiple trips?

Yes! You can make multiple submissions. Each trip you upload is reviewed separately, and you can earn gift certificates for each approved submission.
